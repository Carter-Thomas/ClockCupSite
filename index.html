Tournament Information

Welcome to Clock Cup Season 3, where 24 competitors compete in live, head-to-head, matches in a World Cup style format. The top two competitors in each group from the group stage will automatically advance to the bracket stage, with four wildcards from those who didn't advance automatically.

For qualification results, check the original Clock Cup website, linked here.

Stay tuned for exciting matches and updates!
